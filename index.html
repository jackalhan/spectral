MY PASSION

We live in a world where enormous data are ubiquitous. Therefore, my passion is to make sense of data by using innovative data mining and machine learning algorithms. My current interests are graph mining, latent structure mining, very large graph data management, and bioinformatics.

ACHIEVEMENTS

SCAN, a clustering algorithm for large graphs such as social and biological networks has received over 400 citations since published in ACM SIGKDD’07 according to Google scholar. SCAN is also used by FDA for atBioNet - an Integrated Network Analysis Tool for Knowledge Expansion, Network Modeling and Visualization in Genomics and Proteomics .

DBSCAN, a density-based clustering algorithms has received over 8,000 citations according to Google scholar. DBSCAN is implemented in some most popular data mining and machine learning software including Weka and RapidMiner.. Here is the link to a Wikipedia article about DBSCAN algorithm.

Both SCAN and DBSCAN algorithms are covered by many data mining textbooks.
